# Agent Lesson: 123 Oak St

## Deal Summary
Inherited property from motivated seller. 3/2 ranch, needed full renovation.

## What Went Well
- Responded to lead within 45 minutes
- Seller was motivated (inherited, out of state)
- Used cash_deals_playbook script - seller accepted first offer
- Title was clean

## What Could Improve
- Underestimated repair costs by $12K (hidden foundation issue)
- Should have ordered inspection BEFORE marketing to buyers
- Buyer requested $5K price reduction after their inspection

## Key Takeaway
**Always get inspection before marketing deals.** The $400 inspection cost would have prevented a $5K price renegotiation and preserved our fee.

- Voicemail: "Quick cash offer" script
