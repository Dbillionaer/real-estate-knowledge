# Assignment of Contract for Purchase of Real Estate

**For good and valuable consideration, the sufficiency and receipt of which are hereby acknowledged, the undersigned,**

**[Your Name/Your Company Name]** ("Assignor"),

**hereby assigns, transfers, and sets over unto**

**[Cash Buyer's Name/Cash Buyer's Company Name]** ("Assignee"),

**all of Assignor's rights, title, and interest in and to that certain Real Estate Purchase and Sale Agreement (the "Purchase Agreement") dated [Date of Original Purchase Agreement], between**

**[Seller's Name]** ("Seller"),

**and Assignor, for the purchase and sale of the real property located at:**

**[Property Address], [City], [State], [Zip Code]**

**(the "Property").**

---

**1. Assignment Fee:** Assignee shall pay to Assignor a non-refundable assignment fee in the amount of **$[Assignment Fee Amount]**. This fee is due and payable at the time of the closing of the Purchase Agreement.

**2. Earnest Money:** Assignee shall deposit with the closing agent, within [e.g., 24] hours of the execution of this Assignment, a non-refundable earnest money deposit in the amount of **$[Assignee's Earnest Money Amount]**. This deposit shall be applied to the purchase price at closing. Should Assignee fail to close on the purchase of the Property for any reason other than a default by the Seller, this deposit shall be released to the Assignor as liquidated damages.

**3. Assumption of Obligations:** Assignee hereby assumes all of the Assignor's duties and obligations under the Purchase Agreement and agrees to be bound by all of its terms and conditions. Assignee agrees to perform all covenants, agreements, and obligations required of the buyer under the Purchase Agreement.

**4. "As-Is" Condition:** Assignee acknowledges that they are purchasing the Property in its "as-is, where-is" condition, with all faults, and that Assignor has made no warranties or representations, express or implied, regarding the condition of the Property.

**5. Indemnification:** Assignee agrees to indemnify and hold Assignor harmless from any and all claims, damages, liabilities, and expenses (including reasonable attorney's fees) arising out of Assignee's failure to perform any of the obligations of the buyer under the Purchase Agreement.

**6. Entire Agreement:** This Assignment constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior discussions, negotiations, and agreements, whether oral or written.

**7. Governing Law:** This Assignment shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the State of [Your State].
