# Florida County Hunting Guides

## Top10 Priorities
1. **Miami-Dade**: High competition, focus auctions, probate, tax liens. ARV 400k+, equity targets 50k+.
2. **Broward**: FSBO heavy, driving for dollars suburbs. Check code violations.
3. **Palm Beach**: Luxury distress rare, target vacant land foreclosures.
4. **Hillsborough (Tampa)**: Investor heavy, pre-foreclosure auctions.
5. **Orange (Orlando)**: Tourist rentals seller-finance opportunities.
6. **Duval (Jacksonville)**: Military base motivated PCS sellers.
7. **Lee (Ft Myers)**: Hurricane damage motivated, insurance claims.
8. **Pinellas (St Pete)**: Retiree FSBO, probate goldmine.
9. **Volusia (Daytona)**: Seasonal vacant, tax delinquent.
10. **Brevard (Melbourne)**: Space coast contractors relocating.

## General Rules
- Equity >30k, price <300k
- Sources: BatchData, XLeads, Zillow price-reduced >30d
- Filters: distressed, pre-foreclosure, FSBO, tax delinquent, probate
