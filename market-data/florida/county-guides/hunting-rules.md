# Florida County Hunting Rules

## High Priority Counties (Top 10 by Population/Deals)
- Miami-Dade, Broward, Palm Beach, Hillsborough, Orange, Duval, Pinellas, Lee, Polk, Brevard

### Rules:
1. **Distressed Filters**: Pre-foreclosure (90+ days delinquent), tax liens (>6mo), probate (<6mo), divorce, relocation/military.
2. **Equity Targets**: >$50k equity (ARV 70% rule: Offer = ARV*0.7 - Repairs - $10k wholesale fee)
3. **Price Range**: $100k-$400k single family, duplex/triplex up to $600k.
4. **Sources**: xleads.com, batchdata.com, Zillow FSBO/Auction, PropStream.
5. **County-Specific**:
   - South FL (Miami+): Hurricane damage, insurance claims, condos.
   - Central (Orlando+): Tourism rentals, absentee owners.
   - North (Jacksonville): Military bases, older homes.

## Skip/Low Priority: Rural low-volume counties unless high equity.
